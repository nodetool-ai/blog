## What is NodeTool?

NodeTool is a local-first visual environment for building and running AI workflows. Our mission is to make AI accessible and powerful for everyone, while respecting your privacy and data.

### Key Features

- **Visual Node Editor**: Build complex workflows with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface
- **Local-First**: Your data stays on your machine
- **Multi-Model Support**: Work with various AI providers and models
- **Extensible**: Add your own custom nodes and integrations
